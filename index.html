Fellaz Universe

The universe that is built with IPs processed by Fellaz.

Fellaz aims members to expand their entertainment experience beyond the real world and into the metaverse. Fellaz universe and the IPs within it work as a link that connects the real world with the metaverse. The first project to introduce of the Fellaz universe is ‘Meta Miu’, a community-centered metaverse idol project.

Story of Meta MIU

The girl named Miu, who dreams of becoming an idol. But she goes through a rough time achieving her goal which made her discouraged and consider giving up her dream.

One day, she encounters a cat that resembles Miro, a cat she had left behind in her childhood home 5 years ago. So, she decides to follow it. Miro leads Miu to pass through unfamiliar alleys and Miu finds herself in the world of the metaverse.

In this metaverse world, Felix, a washed-up producer who has been observing Miu, becomes enamored by her potential, so he proposes that she participates in an audition program for metaverse idol.
